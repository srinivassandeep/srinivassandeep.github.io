About

I am a PhD candidate in Information Systems and Operations Management at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research focuses on the intersection of digital platforms, food sustainability, and empirical analysis.

Before pursuing my PhD, I gained five years of industry experience in the banking and finance sector, working at Reliance Industries, Axis Bank, and Yes Bank. This background in data analytics and risk modeling informs my current research on how technology platforms can address real-world challenges in food systems.

My work examines how food delivery platforms impact restaurant market structures, food waste reduction, and food insecurity. I employ empirical methods to understand these complex relationships and their implications for both industry and society.
