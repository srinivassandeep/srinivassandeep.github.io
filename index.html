The Story Behind the Research

From engineering floors to banking towers, now shaping the future of food systems

The Foundation

My journey began in the engineering labs of NIT Calicut, where I learned to solve complex problems with precision. But it was in the fast-paced world of banking—first at Reliance Industries, then Axis Bank and Yes Bank—where I discovered the power of data to transform entire industries.

The Transformation

Seven years of building risk prediction models and analytics solutions taught me something crucial: the most impactful research comes from understanding real-world problems. This realization led me to UIUC, where I'm now pursuing a PhD to tackle one of our era's most pressing challenges—food sustainability.

The Mission

Today, I bridge the gap between academic rigor and industry reality. My research on food delivery platforms, blockchain traceability, and food waste isn't just theoretical—it's informed by years of seeing how technology can reshape markets and create lasting impact.
